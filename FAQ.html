Frequently Asked Questions

What programs does the technical university offer?
The technical university offers a wide range of programs in engineering, computer science, information technology, business, and more. You can find detailed information about each program on our website.
How do I apply for admission?
To apply for admission, you need to fill out the online application form available on our website. Make sure to review the admission requirements and deadlines before applying.
Are there scholarships available for students?
Yes, the technical university offers various scholarships and financial aid programs for eligible students. You can check our scholarship page for more information and application details.
Can international students apply?
Yes, international students are welcome to apply to our university. We have specific admission requirements and support services for international applicants. Please visit our international students page for more information.
What are the tuition fees for the programs?
Tuition fees vary depending on the program and the student's residency status. You can find detailed information about the tuition fees on our tuition and fees page.
Is there on-campus housing available?
Yes, the university offers on-campus housing options for students. Our residential facilities provide a comfortable and convenient living experience. Visit our campus life page to learn more about housing and campus facilities.
