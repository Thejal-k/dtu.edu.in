CRED (Collaborative Research and Experimental Development)

CRED is an initiative that fosters collaboration between researchers and industries. It encourages joint research projects and experimental development to address real-world challenges and promote innovation.

Research Regulations

Our university follows strict research regulations to ensure the integrity and quality of research work. Researchers are required to adhere to ethical guidelines, maintain academic standards, and submit regular progress reports. These regulations are designed to support meaningful and impactful research outcomes.

Places of Research

Deen Dayal Technological University offers state-of-the-art research facilities on campus, including advanced laboratories, well-equipped libraries, and collaborative spaces. Additionally, our university collaborates with various research centers and industries to expand research opportunities for our scholars.

Research Fee

The research fee for pursuing a Ph.D. or other research programs at our university varies based on the chosen field of study. For detailed information about research fees and available financial assistance, please contact our admissions office or visit our website.
